About

What we do:

We provide college football picks for the Spread and Straight-Up bets. Every pick is updated daily until game day so you can be assured you have the most accurate pick possible.

How we do it:

Each day we scrape the web to get the most up to date data available. This information is then fed into our ensemble of machine learning models which in turn provides a non-biased prediction for each and every game. The predicted outcome is captured in the Predicted Outcome field in the Picks Tables.

Picks Table

The current week’s Picks Table is updated daily until game day. Most fields are self-explanatory but for those fields that may be not be so easily understood we've provided explanations below .

Field Definition
Bookie Spread This is the average spread consensus from the most popular online sportsbooks. Negative number favors the home team and vice versa.
Zillion Picks Spread This is our algorithm prediction for the result of the game. Negative number favors the home team and vice versa
Spread Difference The difference between the Vegas Spread and Predicted Outcome. A bigger difference could indicate a bad spread set by Vegas and a betting opportunity but this is not a guarantee.
Finale Game Result Spread The actual result of the game. A negative score means the home team won and vice versa. i.e. -10 means the home team won by 10
Spread Pick Our pick based on the difference between the Vegas Spread and Our Predicted Outcome. i.e. if Vegas Spread -7 and Predicted Outcome -9 we pick the home team or if Vegas Spread -7 and Predicted Outcome -3 we pick the away team
Straight Up Pick Our pick based on the Predicted Outcome. i.e. if Predicted Outcome is -7 we pick the home team or if Predicted Outcome is 7 we pick the away team
Spread / Straight Up Result This indicates if our pick won or lost.
