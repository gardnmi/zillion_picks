About

Why a Zillion Picks? While it's not actually a zillion, our algorithm makes thousands of predictions for each game and then pools those picks together to come up with the best pick possible. Each prediction has been trained with over 500 inputs covering features such as matchup history, game statistics, recruiting performance, roster dynamics and even weather. It's like having a thousand experts collaborate and pick the best possible outcome for each game.  Start Exploring Now!
